Delicious Chocolate Cake

  • Ingredients:
  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 150g butter
  • 150g sugar
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g flour
  1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
  2. Melt the chocolate and butter together in a heatproof bowl over simmering water.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the sugar and eggs until creamy.
  4. Add the melted chocolate mixture to the sugar and eggs, and mix well.
  5. Fold in the flour until just combined.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased cake tin and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  7. Allow the cake to cool before serving.

Classic Caesar Salad

  • Ingredients:
  • 1 head of romaine lettuce
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup Caesar dressing
  1. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce, then tear it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the lettuce, croutons, and Parmesan cheese.
  3. Add the Caesar dressing and toss until well coated.
  4. Serve immediately.
